Our Story

I love coffee!



One day I was debating with my husband, who wasn’t a coffee drink but loves hot chocolate, about my love of coffee. Out of this discussion a challenge evolved. He challenged me to make something that even a non-coffee drinker could enjoy. So, what started out as a simple challenge blossomed into a quest for a perfect coffee drink for all. Knowing that chocolate was HIS WEAKNESS, I immediately leaned towards Mocha. It had to be more than a mocha coffee drink. Then it hit me, maybe a drink wasn’t the answer, maybe a coffee mocha pop was the answer! NEEDLESS TO SAY, AFTER HE CONSUMED 4 FROZEN MOCHAS, we realized the power of a frozen pop and MochaPops was born!!
